What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All round history and shot data stored in the cloud
  • Club dispersion profiles and practice session data
  • Feedback submissions
  • Any other data associated with your account

Local data on your device (rounds, clubs, settings) will remain until you uninstall the App.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to the address below from the email associated with your ShotGain account. Include your account email so we can locate your data. Your account and all associated data will be deleted within 30 days of your request.
